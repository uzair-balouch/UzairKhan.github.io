Vlog Website
Developed in Python Django

This is a Vlog Website for users where user can upload his/her awesome
videos by making an account he can upload videos on his profile and he/she can
also share videos with other social media paltforms.

I have developed this website for users who wanted to make their personal video logs. In this website user have access to their personal profile by making an account where they can enter their personal data which they wanted to show to the world and also they can see the acticity of visitors on their dashboard which only visible to the user.

I have developed dashboard in such a way where they can see how many time their videos viewd by the visitors. They can also edit od delete their videos in the editing option. Their account is been pretected by the password which cannot be stolen because it is authentidated by their personal email.

This website is developed in Python 3.7 and Django 2.1 and all the data of users is been stored in the database. The is been stored in such a way that all the users personal account data iin stores in their account directry and where their email and password is been stored and other data about their videos is stored in the assets directory where useres upload their videos and profile images stc. This website is no on internet yet but it will soon be on online on internet inshaAllah.
